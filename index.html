My dearest Rachel,

On your 26th birthday

I've been sitting here trying to find the right words, and I keep landing on the same truth — that you are one of the greatest things that has ever happened to me. Not just to my life, but to who I am as a person. You make me better simply by being in the room.

I notice it every time I see you light up about something you love, every time you laugh, every time you're just quietly, completely yourself.

I love you in ways I didn't know I was capable of before you. And I am so, so glad you were born.

Happy birthday, Rach. This one's all yours.
