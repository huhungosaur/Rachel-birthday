My dearest Rachel

On your 26th birthday

Happy birthday Rach. 26. I honestly can't believe it.

I've been thinking about what to say and I keep coming back to Singapore. Being somewhere so new, meeting the people who mean the most to you and you just made me feel like I belonged there. Like I'd always been part of it. I don't think I told you enough at the time how much that meant to me, so I'm saying it now.

You do that a lot actually, make things feel easy when they probably shouldn't be. The weekend hikes, the things you plan for us, the way you just quietly take care of everything, including me. You started cooking us proper meals, dragging me to the gym, and somehow I stopped complaining about it and started actually looking forward to it. I don't know how you managed that but here we are.

And that laugh of yours, your little giggle when you're laughing at your own joke before you've even finished telling it. I genuinely live for that. Or when my jokes are so bad that I start laughing at myself and then you just laugh at me laughing. Every time without fail. It gets me.

I'm a better person because of you. Not in some vague way, in real actual ways I can point to. And I don't take that lightly.

Honestly I've just missed you like crazy. Come home soon.

Happy birthday, sweetie. Hope today is as good as you are.

I love you so much, Hunter 🌸
